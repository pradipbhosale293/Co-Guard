What's New
On CO-GUARD ?

This new feature allows fully vaccinated citizens to update
existing photo identity to passport number and date of birth to get
International Travel Certificate.

New feature has been provided for foreign national. You can easily be vaccinated just by providing your basic details. If you have
been administered with Dose 1 vaccine in foreign country, you'll have provide it's details to Vaccinator while going for Vaccination.

This is a new feature provided where you can link your passport by entering Passport number. In order to link your passport to the
certificate you need to login into your CoWIN portal and then raise an issue to get your passport linked by clicking on "Add Passport Details to my vaccination certificate"

You can now get all your Issues related to your Vaccine Certificate at one place. All you need to do is to login to your Co-Guard portal
and click on "Raise an Issue" . Multiple Issue options are provided. You can raise your concerned issues accordingly.s

Citizens administered with dose 1 can now check their eligibility for Dose 2 by performing a single step.
"Login to your Co-GUard portal" and there you have your eligibility mention next to your name, highlighted as "No. of Days left for Dose 2"..
